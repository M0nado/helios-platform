## 📋 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### What Is HELIOS Platform?

HELIOS is a **complete, modular, enterprise-grade Windows optimization ecosystem** that combines:

1. **On-Premise Windows Optimization** (7 versions, starting minimal)
2. **Cloud Integration** (Azure, M365, Entra, Purview, Fabric, Power Platform)
